NovaPay Refund Policy
=====================

Scope
-----
This policy governs all refunds, reversals and transaction disputes on the
NovaPay digital banking app, covering UPI transfers, debit card payments,
cashback, and subscription charges.

1. Failed UPI Transfers (money debited, transfer failed)
--------------------------------------------------------
When a UPI transfer fails but the amount is debited from the customer's
NovaPay account, the amount is auto-reversed within 3 business days. The
customer does not need to file a separate request for an auto-reversal. If the
reversal does not appear after 3 business days, the customer should raise a
dispute from the Transactions screen using "Report an issue". NovaPay credits a
goodwill amount of 50 INR if an auto-reversal exceeds 5 business days, in line
with regulatory turnaround-time (TAT) guidelines.

2. Disputed Charges (unauthorised or incorrect debit)
-----------------------------------------------------
Disputed card or merchant charges are investigated within a 7-day
investigation window from the date the dispute is filed. During investigation,
the disputed amount is NOT provisionally credited unless the transaction value
is below 5,000 INR, in which case a provisional credit is issued within 48
hours. The customer must file disputes within 60 days of the transaction date;
disputes raised after 60 days are reviewed case-by-case and may be declined.

3. Accidental Double Payments
-----------------------------
If a customer is charged twice for the same merchant transaction within a
10-minute window, the duplicate is automatically detected and reversed within
3 business days. If the two charges are more than 10 minutes apart, the
customer must raise a dispute so the merchant can confirm the duplicate.

4. Cashback Eligibility
-----------------------
Cashback is credited within 7 business days of a qualifying transaction.
A transaction qualifies only if: (a) it is 250 INR or more, (b) it is not
later refunded or reversed, and (c) the customer's account is in good standing
(no overdue EMIs). Cashback that is not received within 7 business days can be
claimed via "Report an issue" within 30 days of the transaction. Cashback on
a refunded transaction is clawed back automatically.

5. Refund Timelines Summary
---------------------------
- UPI auto-reversal: 3 business days
- Duplicate payment reversal: 3 business days
- Disputed charge investigation: 7 days
- Provisional credit (under 5,000 INR): 48 hours
- Cashback credit: 7 business days

6. Escalation Path
------------------
If a refund is delayed beyond 10 days from the date it was due, the case is
automatically escalated to a human Refund Resolution Officer. Customers may
also request escalation explicitly at any time. Any disputed amount above
50,000 INR is escalated to human review immediately and is never auto-approved
by an agent. Suspected fraudulent transactions are routed to the Fraud team and
the card is temporarily frozen pending verification.

Important
---------
Support agents must never confirm or approve a refund amount without checking
this policy. If a customer's scenario is not covered here, the agent must say
they need to check with the team rather than inventing a policy detail.
