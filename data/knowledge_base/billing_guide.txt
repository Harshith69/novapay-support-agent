NovaPay Billing & Subscriptions Guide
=====================================

This guide covers EMIs, premium plans, invoices and payment methods. Agents
must quote figures exactly as written here.

1. EMI Deduction Schedule
-------------------------
- EMIs for NovaPay personal loans are auto-debited on the 5th of every month.
- If the 5th falls on a bank holiday or weekend, the debit moves to the next
  business day.
- A reminder notification is sent 3 days before each EMI date.
- If an EMI fails due to insufficient balance, NovaPay retries once after 3
  days. A late fee of 2% of the EMI amount applies if both attempts fail.

2. EMI Deducted Twice
---------------------
- A duplicate EMI debit in the same billing cycle is automatically detected and
  reversed within 5 business days.
- The customer does not need to file a dispute for an auto-reversal, but should
  contact support if the reversal does not appear after 5 business days.
- Agents must never promise a manual instant reversal; the standard timeline is
  5 business days.

3. Premium Plan Pricing Tiers
-----------------------------
- Free: 0 INR/month — basic savings account, UPI, debit card.
- Plus: 199 INR/month — higher UPI limits, 1% cashback, priority chat support.
- Pro: 499 INR/month — everything in Plus, 2% cashback, free airport lounge
  access twice per quarter, dedicated relationship manager.
- Plans are billed on the same calendar date each month as the original signup
  date. A 7-day free trial is available once per customer for Plus and Pro.

4. Cancelling or Downgrading a Plan
-----------------------------------
- Customers can cancel or downgrade anytime via Profile > Subscription > Manage
  Plan. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle; the
  current month is not pro-rated or refunded.
- If a customer is charged for a plan after cancelling, verify the cancellation
  date. If the charge is after the effective cancellation, raise a billing
  dispute for a full reversal.

5. Updating a Payment Method
----------------------------
- Payment methods are managed under Profile > Payments > Saved Methods.
- A newly added card or account becomes the default for the NEXT billing cycle,
  not the current one.
- At least one valid payment method must remain on file for accounts with an
  active loan.

6. Reading the NovaPay Invoice
------------------------------
- Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month and emailed as PDFs.
- Each invoice lists: subscription charges, cashback credited, EMI debits,
  applicable GST (18%), and any late fees.
- Cashback is shown as a credit line and is not taxable.

7. Interest Calculation Disputes
--------------------------------
- Personal loan interest is calculated on a reducing-balance basis at the
  annual rate stated in the loan agreement.
- If a customer disputes interest, agents should explain reducing-balance
  calculation and direct them to the amortisation schedule under
  Loans > My Loan > Schedule. Agents must not recompute or waive interest.

8. High-Value Transactions
--------------------------
Any billing adjustment, waiver, or refund above 50,000 INR must be flagged for
human review and never approved by an agent directly.
